<p><p>The non-invasive sphygmomanometer market includes three main types: Automatic, Mercury, and Aneroid sphygmomanometers. Automatic sphygmomanometers provide digital readings and are user-friendly, making them popular in both home and clinical settings. Mercury sphygmomanometers, considered the gold standard for accuracy, use mercury columns for measurement but are being phased out due to safety concerns. Aneroid sphygmomanometers are mechanical devices that use a dial for readings, often utilized in medical offices for their reliability and portability.</p></p>
