<p><p>The cathode materials market includes binders, foils, and active materials. Binders are adhesive substances that hold active materials together, ensuring structural integrity in batteries. Foils, typically made of aluminum, act as conductive substrates for cathode coatings, facilitating electron flow. Active materials, such as lithium cobalt oxide or nickel-manganese-cobalt, are the core components responsible for energy storage and release. Together, these elements form the foundation of cathode production, driving advancements in battery performance, efficiency, and applications across industries like electric vehicles and renewable energy storage.</p></p>
