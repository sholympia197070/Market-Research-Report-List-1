<p><p>Food tray sealing machines are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operator intervention for sealing, making them cost-effective for small-scale operations. Semi-automatic machines streamline the process, requiring less effort from the operator while increasing efficiency, suitable for medium-scale production. Fully automatic machines offer high-speed, hands-free operation, ideal for large-scale production environments. These machines enhance food preservation, prolong shelf life, and ensure consistent quality in packaging.</p></p>
