<p><p>The Automotive HVAC Controllers market includes two main types: Traditional and Digital. Traditional HVAC controllers use manual knobs and dials to adjust temperature, airflow, and fan speed, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Digital HVAC controllers feature electronic interfaces, touchscreens, or buttons, providing precise control, automation, and integration with advanced vehicle systems. Digital types often include smart features like climate presets and connectivity, enhancing user convenience and energy efficiency, while traditional types remain popular in budget-friendly or older vehicle models.</p></p>
