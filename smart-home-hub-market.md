<p><p>The smart home hub market is primarily divided into Wi-Fi and Bluetooth categories. Wi-Fi hubs connect devices through home routers, enabling broader range and higher data transfer, ideal for streaming and remote access. They facilitate integration of various smart devices seamlessly. Conversely, Bluetooth hubs offer a limited range and lower power consumption, suitable for smaller devices and simple automation tasks. Both types cater to different user needs, with Wi-Fi hubs focusing on extensive connectivity and Bluetooth hubs prioritizing low-energy, localized operations.</p></p>
