<p><p>The rotavator market is primarily categorized into two types: horizontal axis and vertical axis rotavators. Horizontal axis rotavators have blades aligned parallel to the ground, making them effective for soil mixing and tillage in larger fields. In contrast, vertical axis rotavators feature blades positioned upright, which excel at breaking up hard soil and are often used for smaller plots. Both types play a crucial role in modern agriculture, enhancing soil preparation efficiency and improving crop yields.</p></p>
