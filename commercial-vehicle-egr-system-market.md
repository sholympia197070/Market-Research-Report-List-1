<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) System Market comprises two primary types: Electric EGR Valves and Pneumatic EGR Valves. Electric EGR Valves utilize electrical actuators for precise control of exhaust gas flow, enhancing efficiency and responsiveness. In contrast, Pneumatic EGR Valves operate using air pressure to regulate the exhaust gases, typically offering simpler designs and lower costs. Both types contribute to emissions reduction and regulatory compliance in commercial vehicles by recirculating exhaust gases into the intake manifold to lower nitrogen oxide emissions.</p></p>
