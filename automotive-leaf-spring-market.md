<p><p>The automotive leaf spring market includes types like Double End and Open End. Open End leaf springs have one end fixed and the other free, providing flexibility and reduced stress, suitable for lighter vehicles. Both types cater to different vehicle needs, balancing durability, performance, and cost-effectiveness in suspension systems.</p></p>
