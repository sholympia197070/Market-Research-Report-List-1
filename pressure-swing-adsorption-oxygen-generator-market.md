<p><p>A Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) Oxygen Generator is a system designed to produce high-purity oxygen from ambient air by employing a method that utilizes a unique adsorption process. In this technology, compressed air is passed through adsorbent materials that selectively trap nitrogen, allowing oxygen to be separated and collected.
