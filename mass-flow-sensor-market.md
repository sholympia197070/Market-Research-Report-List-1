<p><strong>The Mass Flow Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor</li><li>Hot Film Air Flow Sensor</li><li>Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor</li><li>Vane Air Flow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mass Flow Sensor market includes various types: **Hot Wire Air Flow Sensors** measure airflow using a heated wire, cooling as air passes, providing precise readings. **Hot Film Air Flow Sensors** use a thin film instead of a wire, offering durability and resistance to contamination. **Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensors** detect airflow by measuring vortices created by air passing a bluff body, ensuring high accuracy. **Vane Air Flow Sensors** use a mechanical flap moved by airflow, offering simplicity and reliability for lower-cost applications. Each type caters to specific performance and cost needs.</p></p>
