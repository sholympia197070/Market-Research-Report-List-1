<p><p>The vacuum pumps market comprises various types of pumps tailored for specific applications. Dry vacuum pumps, which function without lubricants, are ideal for clean environments and reduce contamination risks. Liquid ring vacuum pumps utilize a rotating liquid ring to create a vacuum, offering reliable performance for wet applications. Rotary vane vacuum pumps operate using sliding vanes within a rotor, providing consistent vacuum levels and versatility across industries. Each type serves distinct operational needs, enhancing efficiency and reliability in industrial processes.</p></p>
