<p><p>The Veno-Venous Extracorporeal Life Support (VV ECLS) devices market can be categorized into single-ventricular and biventricular devices. Single-ventricular devices are designed to support patients with isolated right heart failure, directing blood flow from the venous system to the lungs for oxygenation. In contrast, biventricular devices provide support for both the left and right heart functions, catering to patients with more comprehensive cardiac failure. Both types are critical for managing severe respiratory and cardiac conditions in critically ill patients.</p></p>
