<p><p>Crude Tall Oil (CTO) is a byproduct of the kraft pulping process used in papermaking, derived from woody biomass. There are two main market types: Softwood Tall Oil and Hardwood Tall Oil. Softwood Tall Oil is extracted from softwood trees like pine and is prized for its higher yield and better chemical composition, making it suitable for industrial applications including surfactants and adhesives. Hardwood Tall Oil, sourced from hardwoods, has a lower yield but offers unique properties for niche applications. Both types are integral to various industries.</p></p>
