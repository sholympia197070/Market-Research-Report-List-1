<p><p>The hydration containers market is segmented into four main types: metal, polymer, glass, and silicone. Polymer containers, typically lightweight and affordable, offer convenience but may lack insulation. Glass containers provide a premium, eco-friendly option, though they are less portable due to fragility. Each type caters to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
