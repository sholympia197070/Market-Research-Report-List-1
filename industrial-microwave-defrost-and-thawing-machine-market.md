<p><p>The industrial microwave defrost and thawing machine market consists of three main types: chambers, tumblers, and continuous lines. Chamber machines operate by placing products in a closed space where microwaves evenly thaw them. Tumblers use rotation and microwave technology to ensure uniform thawing, making them ideal for bulk products. Continuous lines integrate microwave systems into conveyor belts, allowing for a seamless and high-capacity thawing process, suitable for large-scale operations and improving workflow efficiency in food processing.</p></p>
