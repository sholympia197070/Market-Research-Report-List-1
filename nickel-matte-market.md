<p><p>The Nickel Matte market involves various processing methods to extract and refine nickel. **Layered Smelting** separates nickel matte into distinct layers based on density, yielding high-purity nickel. **Leaching** uses chemical solutions to dissolve nickel from ores, followed by recovery. The **Carbonyl Process** converts nickel into a volatile compound, which is then decomposed to produce pure nickel. **Flotation Process** separates nickel-bearing minerals from waste by exploiting differences in surface properties. Each method caters to specific ore types and market demands for nickel products.</p></p>
