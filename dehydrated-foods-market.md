<p><strong>The Dehydrated Foods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray-Dried Foods</li><li>Freeze-Dried Foods</li><li>Vacuum-Dried Foods</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehydrated foods market consists of several types, each with unique preservation methods. Spray-dried foods are produced by rapidly drying a liquid through hot air, resulting in a fine powder, ideal for instant products. Freeze-dried foods undergo freezing and then sublimation, preserving flavor and nutrients, making them popular for camping and emergency supplies. Vacuum-dried foods remove moisture in a low-pressure environment, maintaining texture and color. Other methods may include air-drying or drum-drying, catering to various consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
