<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>E-beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photomask inspection market can be categorized into optical and electron-beam (E-beam) inspection types. Optical inspection utilizes light-based systems to detect defects on photomasks, providing fast and effective quality control for semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, E-beam inspection employs focused electron beams to achieve higher resolution and detect smaller defects with greater accuracy, albeit at slower speeds. Both methods are crucial for ensuring the integrity and performance of photomasks in the advanced semiconductor fabrication process.</p></p>
