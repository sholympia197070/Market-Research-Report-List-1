<p><p>The Double Welded Steel Plastic Geogrid market is categorized into two main types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension. Biaxial tension geogrids are designed to withstand stress in multiple directions, providing enhanced stability and support for various soil reinforcement applications. In contrast, uniaxial tension geogrids are engineered for strength in one direction, making them ideal for specific applications like retaining walls and steep slope stabilization. Both types serve crucial roles in construction and civil engineering by improving load distribution and minimizing soil movement.</p></p>
