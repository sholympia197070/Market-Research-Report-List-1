<p><p>The Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) system is an advanced fuel delivery technology used in internal combustion engines. GDI systems optimize the air-fuel mixture, enabling better combustion and reduced carbon emissions, making them a key component in modern automotive engines.</p><p>The Gasoline Direct Injection System Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for fuel-efficient vehicles and stringent emission regulations worldwide.
