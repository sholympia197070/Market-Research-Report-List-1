<p><p>The laminated wheels market can be categorized into two main types: bonded and non-bonded. Bonded type laminated wheels feature layers of abrasive materials securely attached to a core, ensuring durability and consistent performance. These are ideal for precision applications. In contrast, non-bonded type laminated wheels consist of loosely assembled layers, allowing for flexible use and effective surface finishing. Each type caters to specific needs in industries such as metalworking, automotive, and construction, enhancing efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
