<p><p>Differential Thermal Analyzers (DTA) measure temperature differences between a sample and a reference material to analyze thermal properties. The market includes Analog Signal Output DTA and Digital Signal Output DTA. Analog DTA systems provide continuous voltage signals, offering simplicity and compatibility with older equipment. Digital DTA systems output discrete numerical data, enabling precise analysis, better integration with modern software, and enhanced data processing capabilities. Both types cater to industries like materials science, pharmaceuticals, and polymers, with digital systems gaining traction due to advanced features and accuracy.</p></p>
