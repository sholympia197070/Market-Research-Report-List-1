<p><p>Airport de-icing vehicles are essential for ensuring safe aircraft operations in winter. The **spreader** type disperses solid de-icing agents like sand or salt on runways and taxiways to enhance traction and prevent ice buildup. The **sprayer** type uses liquid de-icing fluids, such as glycol-based solutions, to remove ice and snow from aircraft surfaces. Both types are critical for maintaining operational efficiency and safety, with spreaders focusing on ground surfaces and sprayers targeting aircraft, ensuring smooth airport operations during cold weather.</p></p>
