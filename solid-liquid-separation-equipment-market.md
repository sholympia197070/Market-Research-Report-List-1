<p><p>The solid-liquid separation equipment market encompasses technologies designed to efficiently separate solid particles from liquids. Two key types are centrifugal separators and screw presses. Centrifugal separators utilize high rotational forces to accelerate the separation process, effectively removing solids from liquids. Screw presses, on the other hand, rely on a rotating screw mechanism to compress and dewater solids as liquid is expelled. Both technologies are widely used across various industries, including wastewater treatment, food processing, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
