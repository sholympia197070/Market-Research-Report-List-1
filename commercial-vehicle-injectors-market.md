<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Injectors market includes Gasoline Direct Injectors (GDI) and Diesel Direct Injectors (DDI). GDI systems deliver fuel directly into the combustion chamber of gasoline engines, enhancing efficiency, power, and emissions control. DDI systems, used in diesel engines, inject fuel at high pressure for optimal combustion, improving fuel economy and reducing emissions. Both injector types are critical for modern commercial vehicles, ensuring compliance with stringent emission standards while delivering performance and reliability in heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
