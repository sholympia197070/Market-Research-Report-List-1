<p><p>Field conveyors can be categorized into several types based on their design and application. Roller conveyors use rollers to facilitate the movement of goods, making them ideal for heavy loads. Belt conveyors utilize a continuous belt for transporting materials, suitable for bulk handling. Overhead conveyors suspend goods from above, optimizing floor space. Pallet conveyors are designed specifically for moving palletized loads. Other types may include vibratory or spiral conveyors, each addressing unique material handling needs in various industries.</p></p>
