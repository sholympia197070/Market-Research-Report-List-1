<p><ul><li>Thermoset Elastomers</li><li>Thermoplastic Elastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elastomers for pharmaceuticals market comprises two primary types: thermoset elastomers and thermoplastic elastomers. Thermoset elastomers exhibit excellent chemical resistance and stability, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and heat resistance. In contrast, thermoplastic elastomers offer versatility and easy processing, enabling them to be molded and reshaped without losing their elastic properties. Both types are essential in pharmaceutical packaging, device manufacturing, and drug delivery systems, ensuring safety, efficacy, and compliance in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
