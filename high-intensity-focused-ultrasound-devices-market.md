<p><strong>The High Intensity Focused Ultrasound Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MR-Guided</li><li>Ultrasound-Guided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) devices are categorized primarily into MR-guided and ultrasound-guided types. MR-guided HIFU combines magnetic resonance imaging with focused ultrasound to precisely target tissue while monitoring real-time thermal effects, ensuring higher accuracy in treatment. In contrast, ultrasound-guided HIFU utilizes ultrasound imaging for targeting, offering a more portable and cost-effective solution. Both types are utilized in various therapeutic applications, particularly in oncology, enhancing non-invasive treatment options for tumors and other lesions.</p></p>
