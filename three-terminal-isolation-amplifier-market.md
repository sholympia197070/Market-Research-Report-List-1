<p><p>The three-terminal isolation amplifier market includes photoelectric isolation, capacitive isolation, and electromagnetic isolation. Photoelectric isolation utilizes light to separate electrical circuits, ensuring signal integrity while preventing noise. Capacitive isolation employs capacitors to transmit signals across isolated barriers, making it effective for high-frequency applications. Electromagnetic isolation relies on magnetic fields to achieve signal separation, offering robust immunity to external interference. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing safety and performance in various electronic applications.</p></p>
